Ryan McBride

Alright. Let's get down to business. Syntax Solutions is less than a year old yet our skills and ability are remarkably better that they were when we first began. Although there's still lots to come, we're working hard to keep Material Design the best of the best for design. Using the Materialize Framework in moderation is just how we do that.

Doing that is no easy task; I mean, who doesn't abuse certain aspects of style? Well, to keep Material alive, we're diluting it with bits of other frameworks where we can create the best user experience for our consumers. Layouts, although mostly based off of Google's Material design spec, consist of accent colors and plain whites, greys, and sephia colors.

Keeping things basic was really our mindset creating these templates. We used standard "Lorem Ipsum" text instead of dictating exactly what you should put in a certain place. Our templates are flexible (and not just in terms of screen size). We give you a large enough canvas where you can satisfy your needs but also enough to expand your site. More to come in the future, enjoy!
