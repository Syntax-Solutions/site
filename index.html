What we do

We make templates. Our company isn't a money factory pumping out thousands of templates per day. We at Syntax Solutions craft our code to be responsive, easily modifiable, fun, and fairly complex. We use Materialize CSS along with our own styles. We offer paid and free templates; click the products tab for more information. Enjoy!

Inexpensive

Most of our sites have free options available to you. In our opinion, you should be both satisfied by the way it looks and feels and the price you pay.
HTML5

Our sites are built off of the most compatible framework, HTML5 and CSS3. Alongside Materialize CSS, your website is made to last.
Responsive

All of our sites are responsive. By responsive, we mean that your custom site is optimized for desktops, tablets, and even smartphones.
