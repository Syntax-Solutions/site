Company





Origin

Our company began in December of the year 2015. The moment we decided to launch Syntax Solutions was what inspired us to continue a path towards web design.

Progress

When we first began, we used Bootstrap for all of our templates. Things began to change when our teacher introduced Material design to us. Now, we're working with Materialize CSS to design our sites.

Future

Material design is here to stay (for now at least) and until it goes out of style, we'll be applying it to most of our sites (in moderation, of course).

Accessibility

Thoughtful isn't just a term for the content you put on your site; it's keeping text large enough for people with visual problems like Ryan and Shardul. But it isn't just about us. It's about you. Design

Every time we create a site, we ask ourselves, what's the design of this site that best suites the project goals and consumer's ideas. Color is one of the most important design aspects. What would design be without color? Using Google's color spec, we create an ideal visual experience.
