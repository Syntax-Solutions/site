Jeopardy Solution

Looking for an innovative, modern Jeopardy template? We've got the solution. Create a 6 by 6 grid in a spreadsheet. Fill the top row out with the category names. Then fill out the left-most column with the point values. In each cell, enter the question and answer for each cell. Once you complete this, email this spreadsheet with us at info@syntax-solutions.xyz. We will fill out your template with the questions and answers. When we are done, we will email you the site.
